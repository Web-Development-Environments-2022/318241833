About Me

Muriel Baggee

As a 3-year-old child, Muriel was an extremely mischievous, spoiled, and silly toddler.
As a girl, Muriel was a talented cheerleader and girl scout who performed well in school.
She also attended college, worked as a secretary, and married Eustace at a young age.

Muriel as a toddler

In her later years, Muriel is a devoted housewife with a truly gentle, sweet, and kind demeanor.
She also enjoys cooking, knitting, and quilting.
her desire to join Elisa and Eliza Stitch's quilt club became an obsession.

Adult Muriel

One day Muriel saw a small pink dog in a garbage chute leading into a back alley, she took pity on him and took him home with her, and named him Courage since that day.
Muriel also truly loves her husband, Eustace, though she does not hesitate to discipline him with her rolling pin whenever he crosses the line, such as his deliberately scaring Courage or just being wholly unreasonable or downright malicious
Baby Courage

Muriel & Courage

Eustace Baggee

Eustace was raised in a small household with his deceased father Ickett Bagge (whom he bares a great resemblance to), mother, and his deceased older brother Horst, he was often abused by his mother whom she angrily called "stupid boy", and made fun of him because he was bald.
Adult Eustace

Eustace is a very cynical and jaded old man who is usually irritated and frustrated by the world around him
Eustace has an unquenchable thirst for monetary gain.
He has an adamant disliking for Courage and frequently calls him "stupid dog", angrily taking great pleasure in scaring and messing with him whenever possible, which largely stems out of jealousy that Muriel constantly pampers and dotes on Courage; however, it is not uncommon for Eustace to scare or mess with Courage just for fun or to get Courage to leave him alone.
